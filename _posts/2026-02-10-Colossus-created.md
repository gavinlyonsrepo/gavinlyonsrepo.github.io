Colossus_LTSM is a Python tool for converting TrueType fonts (.ttf) 
into C/C++ bitmap arrays and for visualizing font data stored in C/C++ 
header files. It is aimed at users working with embedded systems, 
LCDs, and GUIs where compact fonts are needed.

* [Colossus_LTSM link](https://github.com/gavinlyonsrepo/Colossus_LTSM)
