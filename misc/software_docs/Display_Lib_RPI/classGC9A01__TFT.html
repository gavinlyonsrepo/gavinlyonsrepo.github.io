GC9A01 power states based on the power control flow chart. These states combine the modes: Normal/Partial, Idle ON/OFF, and Sleep IN/OUT. They are listed from highest to lowest power consumption. Power states are based on the power control flow chart in the datasheet. FIG 89 5.10.2. Power Flow Chart.
